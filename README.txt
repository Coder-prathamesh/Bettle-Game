# Bettle-Game

Beetle is a simple dice game for one player. The aim is to collect all the body parts of a beetle. Rolling an unbiased six-sided dice, a 1 gives a beetle body, 2 gives a head, 3 gives an antenna, 4 gives an eye, 5 gives a mouth, and 6 gives a leg. 
#Game Rules:
1)The player must collect a beetle body before any other body parts can be added.
2)must collect a beetle head before any other parts like antennae, eyes or mouth can be added.
3)complete beetle consists of one body, one head, two antennae, two eyes, one mouth, and six legs.complete beetle consists of one body, one head, two antennae, two eyes, one mouth, and six legs.
